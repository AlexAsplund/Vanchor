Distinct sound per severity, sonar ping for depth:
All sounds are synthesized on the device — nothing to download, works offline. Browsers unlock audio on your first tap.
Touch / haptics
Short pulse on taps, heavier on STOP, a distinct buzz on safety alarms, and a confirmation pulse when a press-and-hold registers. Android phones/tablets; iOS has no vibration API.
Notifications
Subscribed browsers—
Delivered via your browser's push service — the boat needs internet at send time (phone hotspot or dock WiFi). Requires HTTPS; on iOS add the app to your Home Screen first (iOS 16.4+).
