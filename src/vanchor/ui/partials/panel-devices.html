Serial ports

            
Pick a detected port. Prefer a "stable" entry (/dev/serial/by-id/… or an on-board /dev/serial0) — it survives reboots & replugging; a raw /dev/ttyUSB0 can get renumbered. Set each port's baud to match its device.

Records your sensors' noise while the boat sits completely still with the motor off, then tunes the fusion filter to match. Re-run it whenever you move or replace a sensor.
